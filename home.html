About product
We are planning to implement a device that can predict cardiovascular diseases (heart diseases) before it happens . Our product is based on not only one factor but also it takes several inputs to predict a accurate answer to the person who are going to used it. That’s the specialty of our upcoming product. In present world there are many existing products and devices to detect heart problems, but they are not gives a fully accurate answer to predict patient’s health situation. Our product is not based on one parameter. Its based on several parameters. For that we can generate a highly accurate health report regarding heart diseases. Our device is gives a output that high percentage equal as doctor’s live prediction. But we have to mention that our product is not for replacing doctor’s procedure. Our main aim is save patient’s life when the patient feels unsecure at outside of hospitals. Patient can use this device and get instant actions for a medical help and also our device informs family members of patient’s in real time by sending alerts. Now we are going to implement new innovation to save more of mankind however we can. We are going to use present innovations with and trying to build more accurate IOT device to predict cardiovascular diseases in real time. Before we are going to talk about this device, we have focused with what are the real parameters that doctors are diagnosing to get idea about heart patient.
